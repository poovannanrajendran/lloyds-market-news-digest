  • Cygenesis framework: recommends a multi-dimensional cycle analysis that combines threat intelligence, capital flows and evolving loss patterns (drawing parallels with property-cat and D&O cycles) because turning points are typically multi‑factor and can be triggered by moderate, accumulation or event-driven losses rather than a single extreme event.
