LexisNexis Risk Solutions introduces AI-powered Location Intelligence tool for US home insurers - Reinsurance News

Source: www.reinsurancene.ws · Score: 0.72
Why it matters: AI-based location risk scoring and underwriting workflow for homeowners insurance; industry insurtech trend.
  • LexisNexis launched Location Intelligence for Home — an AI/neural‑network predictive analytics product integrated into its Smart Selection platform to support underwriting at new business and renewal stages.
  • The model combines industry‑wide home claims, location‑based intelligence and historical loss trends to generate property‑level risk scores; homes with the highest scores are reported as ~20× more likely to incur a claim than those with the lowest.
  • Risk scoring covers six exposures (hail, wind, weather‑related water, non‑weather water, freeze events, and collapse/falling objects) with optional roof‑condition grading, explicitly targeting gaps in traditional exterior inspections and crediting non‑weather water (≈24% of claims) as a key driver.
  • Solution provides portfolio‑level visibility, operational integration to standardize underwriting decisions, eliminates the need to build/maintain in‑house predictive models, and will be filed for use in multiple US states.
