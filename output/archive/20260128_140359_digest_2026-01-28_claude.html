Lloyd's Market Executive Digest

2026-01-28 · Executive Briefing

Executive summary

The Lloyd's market and global specialty insurance sector are experiencing significant developments, driven by innovation, growth, and strategic partnerships. Key trends include the expansion of insurance-linked securities, new syndicates, and increased use of London Bridge 2 PCC. The sector has seen a 22.3% net total return in 2025, with $660m of new capital channeled into the marketplace. Regulatory updates, technological advancements, and shifting global trends are also shaping the landscape. Brokers, syndicates, and placement platforms must adapt to remain competitive. The sector is poised for further growth and transformation, driven by InsurTech and digitalization.
