Here's a summary of recent catastrophe bond deals from the provided information:

1. **Matterhorn Re Ltd. (Series 2026-1)**
   - Issuer: Swiss Re
   - Type: Fifteenth takedown under its Matterhorn Re program.
   - Target Size: Undisclosed, but aimed at broad aggregate and second or subsequent event retrocessional catastrophe protection for peak North American perils.
   - Notes: Three tranches of Series 2026-1 cat bond notes will be offered to investors.

2. **Gateway Re Ltd. (Series 2026-1)**
   - Issuer: SageSure, a coastal property managing general underwriter
   - Type: Twelfth issuance in the Gateway Re series.
   - Target Size: $765 million for broad-based multi-peril reinsurance protection.
   - Notes: A single tranche of Series 2026-1 Class A notes will be sold to investors.

3. **Nature Coast Re Ltd. (Series 2026-1)**
   - Issuer: SafePoint Insurance Company
   - Type: Ninth catastrophe bond sponsorship.
   - Target Size: Single tranche of Series 2026-1 Class A notes.
   - Notes: Will provide multi-year reinsurance protection to SafePoint and its reciprocal exchanges in Louisiana and Florida.

4. **Sanders Re III Ltd. (Series 2026-1B)**
   - Issuer: Allstate
   - Type: Seeking additional broad multi-peril catastrophe reinsurance.
   - Target Size: $250 million across two tranches of notes, although individual tranche sizes are not yet known.
   - Notes: Designed to provide per-occurrence coverage.

5. **Sanders Re IV Ltd. (Series 2026-1A)**
   - Issuer: Allstate
   - Type: Similar to Sanders Re III, aiming for additional broad multi-peril catastrophe reinsurance.
   - Target Size: $250 million across two tranches of notes, although individual tranche sizes are not yet known.
   - Notes: Designed to provide per-occurrence coverage.

Each of these deals involves the issuance of catastrophe bonds aimed at providing insurance or reinsurance companies with protection against various risks, primarily natural disasters in North America.
