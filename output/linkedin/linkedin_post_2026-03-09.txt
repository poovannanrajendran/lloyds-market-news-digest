London Market signal: Distribution controls, reinsurance towers and capital‑market capacity are shifting — and that has direct consequences for brokers, syndicates and platforms.

Why it matters: These items affect where risk sits, how placements are routed and who needs stronger onboarding, analytics and ILS capability to protect clients and capital.

1) ALTPREMIUM (altpremium.com) FCA warning - Why it matters: An FCA-flagged unauthorised intermediary creates immediate counterparty, payment‑diversion and regulatory/reputational risk for brokers, syndicates and placement platforms.
2) Liberty Mutual lifts aggregate reinsurance attachment point - Why it matters: Moving aggregate protection higher means carriers retain more tail exposure, altering pricing, retentions and demand for bespoke aggregate capacity.
3) Aon names George Attard Global Head of Analytics, Reinsurance - Why it matters: Upgraded broker analytics will drive placement decisioning and capital allocation, forcing syndicates and platforms to integrate data and workflows to stay competitive.
4) Olympus sponsors $100m Abacab Re Florida wind cat bond - Why it matters: A regional specialist sourcing multi‑year ILS capacity increases capital‑markets competition and requires brokers/syndicates to coordinate ILS structuring alongside traditional treaty placement.
