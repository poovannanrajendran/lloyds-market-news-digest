London Market signal: Treasury plumbing, capital sourcing and underwriting leadership are already reshaping placement and liquidity choices.

1) DTCC cleared tri‑party enhancements - Why it matters: Changes to cleared tri‑party repo and margin mechanics will alter demand for HQLA, repo financing and collateral reuse, shifting liquidity profiles and capital allocation for syndicates and brokers.

3) AM Best: Cat bonds more favourable in some layers - Why it matters: Evidence that cat bonds can be more economic than reinsurance in parts of the stack will prompt syndicates and brokers to evaluate ILS as a capital‑efficient alternative for selected layers.
