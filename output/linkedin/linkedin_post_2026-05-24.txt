Signal from the London Market: H1 2026 is a decisive reset in specialty risk transfer.

Cat‑bond issuance and rising ILS allocations, together with broker‑engineered blended placements, are compressing reinsurance pricing and reallocating capacity — a strategic and operational challenge for syndicates, brokers and placement platforms.

3) State Farm $1.5bn Merna Re private cat bond - Why it matters: Large incumbents are using private/club ILS to secure scale protection, fragmenting distribution and reducing opportunities for public syndicates.
4) Patriot Select $310m tower with explicit second‑event protection - Why it matters: Hybrid towers with second‑event cover are becoming material in multi‑event jurisdictions, so brokers must price and document second‑event terms explicitly.
