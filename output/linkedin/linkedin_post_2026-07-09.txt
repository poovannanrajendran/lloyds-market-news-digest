Signal for the London Market: Regulator scrutiny, impersonation scams and a fresh wave of alternative capital are changing how we underwrite, place and finance risk.

Why it matters: Firms need to tighten data and conduct controls, harden payment/onboarding checks, and rethink capital timing and ILS use to protect margins and client flows.

1) FCA emphasis on data & technology - Why it matters: The FCA’s push for data, tech and transparency means firms must accelerate data governance and explainability to satisfy supervisory requests and defend pricing models.
2) algebriscapital.com clone warning - Why it matters: Clone impersonation increases the likelihood of misdirected premiums, fraudulent invoices and compromised client relationships for brokers and placement platforms.
3) ILS issuance to exceed $20bn (Aon) - Why it matters: Renewed ILS supply will compete with traditional reinsurance capital, forcing syndicates to refine layering and pricing strategies.
4) CSU lowers Atlantic hurricane forecast to “well below normal” - Why it matters: A reduced near‑term cat outlook can temper reinsurance pricing momentum and create windows to redeploy capital or time placements.
