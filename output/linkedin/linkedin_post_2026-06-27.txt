3) $75m Woody Re 2026-1 cat bond for Fidelis (Syndicate 3123) - Why it matters: Shows syndicates accessing capital markets directly, providing a tool to manage volatility and capital requirements at syndicate level.
4) Record heatwave knocked out power for 100,000 - Why it matters: Highlights non‑modelled accumulation and business‑interruption exposures, increasing demand for parametric/index solutions and tighter aggregation controls.
