Why it matters: Legal ruling on workers' compensation findings' impact on personal injury litigation; affects liability exposure and underwriting for insurers.
  • NY Court of Appeals: Workers’ Compensation Board findings cannot be used to automatically preclude related personal‑injury lawsuits (Waldy Quinones Garcia v. Monadnock Construction).
  • Justice for Injured Workers Act (effective Dec. 30, 2022) applies prospectively to pending and future civil suits as of that date — even when the underlying comp board decision predates the Act.
