Nexco Assets

Source: fca.org.uk
Why it matters: FCA warning on 'Nexco Assets' is a direct reminder that unauthorised actors target insurance and investment channels; brokers, MGAs and placement platforms face operational and reputational exposure if they or their clients transact with such firms.
  • Immediate review of onboarding and counterparty screening processes for third‑party platforms and non‑standard intermediaries.
  • Communicate to clients the limits of FSCS and FOS protections where counterparties are unauthorised; update placement terms to mitigate recovery expectations.
  • Escalate suspicious activity reporting and coordinate with capacity providers and reinsurers on exposure limits and due diligence expectations.
