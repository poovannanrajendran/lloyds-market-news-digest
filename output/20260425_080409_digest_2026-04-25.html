IoT sensors and satellite imagery to play key role in future of parametric flood insurance: Swiss Re - Artemis.bm

Source: www.artemis.bm · Score: 0.80
Why it matters: Discusses parametric flood insurance, sensor tech, and data sources shaping flood risk transfer—topics of interest to Lloyd's market news.
  • Advanced sensor and remote‑sensing technologies (IoT flood sensors, SAR, satellite imagery, LiDAR and integrated hydrologic models) now enable direct, high‑resolution observation of floods and more accurate parametric triggers.
  • Flood is a widespread, highly correlated peril (affecting ~1/3 of the global population) with a large protection gap—roughly 80% of flood losses remain uninsured—creating systemic exposure for insurers and reinsurers.
  • Parametric flood insurance, which provides rapid, predefined payouts, is gaining traction as tech integration reduces basis risk and improves confidence; this trend is driving partnerships between IoT providers, modelers and carriers.
  • Key challenges: single‑source data creates vulnerabilities, historical proxy‑based indices lacked precision, and data costs remain high. Industry standards, multisource integration, and cross‑sector collaboration are needed to lower costs, improve replicability and scale parametric flood solutions.
