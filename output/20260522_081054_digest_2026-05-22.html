Why it matters: Regulatory update on third-country branches and reporting reforms that could affect Lloyd's market operations.
  • PRA finalised CP20/25 in a Policy Statement, updating the Rulebook and multiple supervisory statements and statements of policy (eg SS44/15, SoP7/24, SoP6/24, SS41/15, SoP1/19, SS19/16) to implement the changes for third‑country branches, and published feedback from consultation responses.
