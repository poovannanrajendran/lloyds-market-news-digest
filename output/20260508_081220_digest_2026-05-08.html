Briefing: The industry needs to stop treating cyber insurance as optional

Source: www.insurancetimes.co.uk · Score: 0.72
Why it matters: Discusses cyber insurance market dynamics and SME uptake, a relevant topic for Lloyd's market news.
  • SMEs remain sceptical and underinsured: fewer than one in four have cyber cover (microbusinesses are even lower), despite being prime targets for simple attacks like phishing, invoice fraud and stolen credentials.
  • Cyber is a primary business risk, not a specialist add‑on: email, online banking, cloud systems and supplier networks can halt trading, drain accounts, trigger regulation and cause reputational damage that small firms often cannot absorb.
