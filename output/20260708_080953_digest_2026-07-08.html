Australia's APRA makes access to alternative reinsurance (cat bonds and ILS) simpler - Artemis.bm

Source: www.artemis.bm · Score: 0.68
Why it matters: Australian APRA reinsurance/ILS changes (incl catastrophe bonds) could affect global reinsurance markets and Lloyd's market participants.
  • APRA has finalised amendments to its prudential framework to improve insurer access to alternative reinsurance and ILS (notably catastrophe bonds), aiming to reduce regulatory burden while maintaining policyholder safeguards.
  • Reinstatement requirements will not be mandated where reinstatements are typically unavailable (eg, cat bonds); insurers must document how they will manage the absence of reinstatement in their Reinsurance Management Strategy (ReMS).
  • APRA will not impose additional capital requirements solely because a product lacks reinstatement; assessment will consider risk management, commercial factors and capital together rather than prescribing extra capital for alternative structures.
