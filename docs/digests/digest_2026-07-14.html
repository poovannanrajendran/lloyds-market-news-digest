Executive summary

Europe's move to T+1 settlement by 2027 creates an urgent operational and strategic imperative for the Lloyd's market, global specialty carriers, brokers, syndicates and placement platforms. Middle-office processes that have historically tolerated T+2 timing—reconciliations, premium and commission flows, inter-syndicate transfers and platform settlement workflows—must be automated, reconfigured and fully tested with counterparties and vendors before industry testing begins in early 2027.
