FCA and the Bank of England set out approach to joint regulation of systemic stablecoin issuers

Source: fca.org.uk
Why it matters: Joint FCA–Bank of England supervision of systemic stablecoin issuers changes the prudential and resolution landscape for payment and settlement rails that could be used for premium collection, claims settlement, collateral and tokenised asset custody across Lloyd's syndicates, brokers and placement platforms.

  • Contracting and operational change: Placement platforms, brokers and custodians should update onboarding, contractual credit terms, reconciliations and dispute-resolution clauses to reflect a potential change in issuer supervision and legal certainty.
