Source: fca.org.uk
Why it matters: Northbank Private Banking appears on the FCA warning list as an unauthorised firm—this creates direct counterparty, payment routing and reputational risk for brokers, placement platforms and syndicates if clients or counterparties use it to move premium or claims funds.
