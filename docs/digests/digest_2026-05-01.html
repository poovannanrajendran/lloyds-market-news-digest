Source: fca.org.uk
Why it matters: FCA warning about an unauthorised "Mortgage Advisor" underscores persistent risks from unregulated intermediaries and fraud that can touch insurance distribution, placement platforms and broker networks — exposing syndicates and managing agents to reputational, operational and compensatory liabilities.
