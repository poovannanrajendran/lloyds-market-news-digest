Executive summary

Lloyd's and the global specialty market are at an inflection: broker revenue momentum (Aon/Marsh) is funding platform scale while syndicates accelerate cyber and consortium product innovation. Geopolitical conflict, sanctions and heightened enforcement are tightening wordings, collateral controls and compliance across brokers, placement platforms and capacity providers. Growth in ILS and alternative capital is broadening placement options even as cyber shows rate moderation, reshaping margin…
