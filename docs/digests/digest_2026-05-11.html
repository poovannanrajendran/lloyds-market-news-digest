Top networking tips for 2026’s Biba Conference

Source: insurancetimes.co.uk
Why it matters: Although framed as general networking advice, the guidance is directly applicable to brokers, syndicates and placement platforms operating in Lloyd's and global specialty markets. The conference is a concentrated environment to identify underwriting appetite, accelerate introductions that lead to slips and binds, evaluate platform readiness for onboarding, and recruit/retain experienced placement talent.
  • Pre-event targeting: map priority syndicates, lead underwriters and platform partners; circulate concise risk summaries and proposed terms in advance to secure scheduled one-to-one meetings and shorten underwriting cycles.
