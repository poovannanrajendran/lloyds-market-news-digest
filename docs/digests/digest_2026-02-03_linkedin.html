Munich Re launches pandemic consortium with parametric focus at Lloyd's - Reinsurance News

Source: reinsurancene.ws
Why it matters: Munich Re’s Pandemic Consortium at Lloyd's, with an objective parametric trigger design, is a leading example of syndicate-level innovation in transferable pandemic risk and placement efficiency.
  • Parametric structure reduces basis and claims settlement friction, offering quicker pay-outs attractive to corporate buyers and placement platforms
  • Establishes a Lloyd's-hosted template for catastrophic communicable disease coverage that other syndicates may replicate or co-participate in
  • Will influence brokers’ advice on pandemic risk transfer and create precedent for objective triggers in other emerging risk products

Radian completes $1.67bn acquisition of Lloyd’s syndicate Inigo - Reinsurance News

Source: reinsurancene.ws
Why it matters: Radian's $1.67bn acquisition of Lloyd's syndicate Inigo represents material capital redeployment into Lloyd's capacity and marks a significant consolidation that will alter competitive dynamics for syndicates, brokers and placement platforms.
  • Brings substantial Lloyd's underwriting capability under a US-listed balance sheet, increasing competition for specialty lines and influencing capital allocation decisions
  • May catalyse further syndicate M&A and strategic exits/entries, requiring brokers and platforms to adapt placement strategies and counterparty assessments
  • Impacts retrocession, reinsurance purchasing and treaty structures as new ownership aligns Lloyd's underwriting with global distribution priorities
