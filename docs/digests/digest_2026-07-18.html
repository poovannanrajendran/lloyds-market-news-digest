Regulators propose UK captive insurance regime

Source: globalreinsurance.com
Why it matters: The PRA and FCA’s proposed UK captive regime aims to make the UK a competitive domicile by offering a tailored regulatory framework and accelerated authorisation. For Lloyd's market participants, brokers and placement platforms, the consultation creates opportunities to capture captive formation, management and reinsurance-placement work while altering retained-risk strategies and ceded-reinsurance demand.
